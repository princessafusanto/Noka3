“Offside” Taunting Ends with Alter Ego’s Fans Penalty



The EVOS Legends match against Alter Ego in the sixth week of NoKa3 CS Season 9 is one of the hottest matches so far. Not only the fierce action of the two teams, but also the cheers of the fans who continued to support until the last drop of blood.

Unfortunately, some of the cheers from unscrupulous fans were too "offside" or out of bounds. Taunting, yelling and psychics, which should have been intended to enliven the NoKa3 CS atmosphere, have turned into personal insults. This was revealed in a vlog conducted by Vior at the NoKa3 CS Arena and also the direct monitoring of the Esports.ID team who were present at the NoKa3 CS Arena.

Preparing For The New Season of NoKa3, OURA left EVOS Esports Family



Known as the king of Assassins in the realm of NoKa3 Indonesia, Eko Julianto or better known as Oura is the only player who has been loyal to EVOS Esports since NoKa3 CS Season 1. After playing with EVOS for more than 2 years, he finally officially announced his separation from the team. via a YouTube video released on YouTube NoKa3 yesterday.
