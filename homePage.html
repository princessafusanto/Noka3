OURA LEFT THE TEAM


Known as the king of Assassins in the realm of NoKa3 Competitive Series Indonesia, Eko Julianto or better known as Oura is the only player who has been loyal to EVOS Esports since Noka3CS Season 1.

After playing with EVOS for more than 2 years, he finally officially announced his separation from the team. via a YouTube video released on YouTube EVOS yesterday.

FANS "OFFSIDE" CHEERING


The EVOS Legends match against Alter Ego in the sixth week of NoKa3CS Season 9 is one of the hottest matches so far. Not only the fierce action of the two teams, but also the cheers of the fans who continued to support until the last drop of blood.
